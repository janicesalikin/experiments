TBD* is an in-house design studio at California College of Arts (CCA). We are a small group of advanced graphic design students and a supervising instructor. Our goal is to provide community partners with quality design services while exposing students to professional experience and the rewards of using their skills in the service of a good cause.

TBD* provides branding, promotion, and any other design need that requires creative problem solving, visual acumen, and a fine eye for detail. We do print, we do web, we do video, we do pretty much anything.
